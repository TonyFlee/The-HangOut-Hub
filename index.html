About

Our bot is designed to enhance your Discord server with fun and interactive features. From an in-depth economy system to engaging roleplay and gambling commands, we offer something for everyone. Whether you're looking to manage your community more efficiently or just add some extra fun, this bot is for you.

  • Automated moderation
  • 24/7 uptime with low latency
  • Customizable prefix and commands

Features

Economy

Manage virtual currencies, create stores, and set up customizable rewards for your server members.

Gamble

Engage in fun gambling activities like coin flips, slot machines, and more to win big in-game rewards.

Roleplay

Build your character, participate in adventures, and immerse yourself in a fully-featured roleplay system.

Utility

Useful tools like polls, reminders, and customizable alerts help keep your server running smoothly.

FAQ

How do I invite the bot to my server?

Click the "Invite" button above to add the bot to your server. You will need to have "Manage Server" permissions.

What are the bot’s default permissions?

The bot requires permissions to manage messages, read message history, and more. You can customize permissions during the invite process.

How do I report bugs or request features?

You can join our support server by clicking the "Support" button above and reporting any issues you encounter or suggesting new features.
